Biography

I am currently working as an ML Engineer at TEKsystems (HP R&D), Bangalore, India. Prior to joining TEKsystems, I worked at MachineSense, Kolkata. Before that, I was a PhD student in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at IIT Kharagpur, India. My PhD research was focused on machinery fault diagnosis and prognosis using deep learning. At IIT Kharagpur, I was advised by Prof. A. R. Mohanty. During my PhD, I developed an interest in machine learning and deep learning in particular. Though my training is in mechanical engineering, I have acquired machine learning skills by self-study and from MOOCs through online certifications. Beyond research, I like literature and music.
